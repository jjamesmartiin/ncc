Intro to Climbing Class

$50 per 1-hr session, up to 4 sessions

Our Intro to Climbing Class is geared towards climbers that have found a new passion for climbing and want to start the journey towards advanced climbing. Our four class course will teach beginners about technique, foot work, gear, etiquette and more. Free day pass and rental gear included!

In this course you will learn:

  • Top rope and bouldering technique
  • How to properly use climbing gear
  • Belaying, lowering, and communication

Gym Lead Class

$120 ~3-hour session

For those climbers that have honed their skills on top rope, we offer an intro to lead climbing class. This one-on-one course will provide everything you need to know to start Leading. Discounted to $100 per person if you bring your climbing partner!

In this course you will learn:

  • Important knots for climbing
  • Safe lead belaying using a GriGri
  • Safe and efficient clipping techniques
  • How to handle falls as a belayer
  • How to safely fall

Prerequisites:

  • Bring your own sport climbing harness
  • Must be able to comfortably climb 5.9 on top rope

Gym to Crag Class

$120 ~3-hour session

Learn to climb outdoor sport routes in our one-on-one Gym to Crag Class! Join us for 3-4 hours covering Anchor Building, Rappelling, Cleaning and Self Rescue Basics, plus a more in-depth look at the necessary gear. Discounted to $100 per person if you bring your climbing partner!

In this course you will learn:

  • How to safely build and equalize anchors
  • How to clean anchors and rappel
  • Outdoor safety protocol
  • Basic self rescue
  • Proper rope and gear management

Prerequisites:

  • Completion of our Gym Lead Class or equivalent experience
  • Bring your own sport climbing harness
  • Must be able to comfortably climb 5.9 on top rope
